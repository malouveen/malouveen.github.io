About Me

My name is Malou Veen van Wolfswinkel and I am currently a second year student at University College Utrecht. I am interested in the application of GIS and remote sensing techniques mostly for environmental analysis, as this is my academic area of focus. I was born in Tarapoto,Peru and grew up in the Hague, The Netherlands, so I decided to focus on these regions for a few of the GIS projects. This course was a great opportunity to learn about the many possibilities of GIS, and I am excited to show you my progress in this field so far.
